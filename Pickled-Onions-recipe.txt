Ingredients:
 - 1 medium red onion, thinly sliced
 - 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
 - 1 cup warm water
 - 1 tablespoon sugar
 - 1-1/2 teaspoon Kosher salt


Directions:
 1. Combine vinegar, water, sugar, and salt in a bowl. Whisk until dissolved.
 2. Place onion in jar. Pour in vinegar mixture. Let sit 1 hour at room temp.
 3. Place in refrigerator for 24 hours.
 4. Will keep for 2-3 weeks.
